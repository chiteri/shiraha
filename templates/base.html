History of the Welfare Association

Posted by Sam | Filed under History, About

Shiraha Welfare association and investment group was started in the late 1970s as an effort to take developments back at home. ^^^. This was by its three esteemed members Mr. Fred Andayi Akolo, Mr. Evans Mukolwe and Mr. James Opindi. They are the first officials of the group who acted as its Chairman, secretary and treasurers respectively.

Originally the meetings used to be held at hotels and restaurants around the city but this proved to be a little prohibitive to some of the members who had lesser disposable incomes at the time. It was then decided to be holding these in individuals' houses. .
